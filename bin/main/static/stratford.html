Stratford Review

Star Rating: 3 / 5

#Active

Points of Interest:

  • Stratford Shakespeare Festival
  • Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory

Stratford is a gorgeous little town just north of the border. If you are able to visit during the annual Shakespeare festival you'll be treated to a plethora of wonderful stage performances, and if that is not quite your style I must recommend the quiet joy of a candied apple while strolling the river Avon.
