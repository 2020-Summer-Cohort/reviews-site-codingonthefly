Jeju Island Review

Star Rating: 5 / 5

Points of Interest: Jungmun Lava Columns, Museum of Glass

Jungmun Lava Columns

These columns are located near the Mangnan Lava Tubes. They are formed by volcanic lava being cooled by the ocean water, naturally taking the striking column shape. They also have "Black Sand Beaches" nearby, which are beachfronts that have black sand, instead of the usual tan.

Museum of Glass

The Museum of Glass is exactly what it sounds like. It is a museum that houses thousands of blown glass art pieces. Some reference old tales like jack and the bean stalk, while others reference Cinderella. Largely most of them are scenic views, like trees and flowers.
