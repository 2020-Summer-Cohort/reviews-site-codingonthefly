Gettysburg Review

Star Rating: 4 / 5

#History #Active

Point of Interest:

  • Gettysburg National Military Park
  • The Links At Gettysburg

Gettysburg National Military Park is rated number one on things to do in Gettysburg, Pennsylvania, this historic military landmark was once a major turning point in the Civil War. Thought to have been one of the bloodiest battles during the Civil War, this was also a pivotal moment in history that gave inspiration for Abraham Lincoln’s “Gettysburg Address”. Many locals say that this landmark is also extremely haunted. For anyone who is an avid golfer, the Links At Gettysburg links-style course presents a demanding layout with unforgiving fairways and greens, as well as various steep rock cliffs, lakes, and waterfalls spanning across its 18 holes. At a length of 7069 yards, this par-72 course is one of the larger public courses in the area, making it a challenge for some golfers off the tee. After the turn, or at the end of your round, The Links At Gettysburg also has a spacious indoor/outdoor bar with great food and beverages.
