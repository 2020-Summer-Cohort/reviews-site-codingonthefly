Cinque Terre Review

Star Rating: 5 out of 5

Points of Interest:

  • Cinque Terre Trail
  • Boat Taxi

We got to Cinque Terre and our mouths dropped. They had breath taking views. There were onl three ways to travel in between the cities. First was hiking the Cinque Terre Trail. Second was the Boat Taxis and last the was the train. We highly recommend the hike.
