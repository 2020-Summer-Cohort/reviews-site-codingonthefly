Gettysburg National Military Park

Rated number one on things to do in Gettysburg, Pennsylvania, this historic military landmark was once a major turning point in the Civil War. Thought to have been one of the bloodiest battles during the Civil War, this was also a pivotal moment in history that gave inspiration for Abraham Lincoln's "Gettysburg Address". Many locals say that this landmark is also extremely haunted.
